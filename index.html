// 01 — World & Lore

Two Worlds.
One City.

Beneath the surface of an ordinary modern city exists a hidden world — one shaped by an ancient dimension known as The Veil. A primordial, infinite forest-like plane that bleeds supernatural energy into our world, influencing the evolution of creatures that now live among humans in disguise.

"The Veil is not heaven. Not hell. It is something far older — a wild, unstable ecosystem that the world was never meant to forget."

These beings — called The Veiled — pass as humans every day. Three forms: a perfect disguise, a semi-revealed state visible only to a few, and a full revelation no one can ignore. Most humans never suspect a thing.

You are one of the rare few who can see through it all.

🌲 About The Veil

A superior dimension resembling an infinite primordial forest, constantly leaking ancient energy into the human world. It shaped the myths humanity created — the Garden of Eden, the Elysian Fields, sacred groves.

Veil Rifts — artificial tears between dimensions — can be opened through forbidden rituals, unleashing Veil Beasts, creating Familiars, or granting enormous power to those willing to pay the price.

Royals who use their full power leave a supernatural signature — a mark detectable across the Veil. A warning. Or an invitation.

The Veil does not belong to good or evil. It simply is.

Factions of the Hidden World

⚫
The Grimms
Hunter · Legend · Terror

Almost myth to most Veiled. Their eyes turn completely black during a hunt — and in that darkness, creatures see their own predatory nature reflected back. Pure instinctive terror. Stronger, faster, built to hunt.

👑
The Royals
Bloodline · Power · Manipulation

Pureblooded Veiled nobility with a deeper connection to the Veil. Their power far exceeds common Veiled — but using it fully leaves a supernatural mark that makes them detectable across dimensions.

🕶️
Council of Silence
Control · Secrecy · Government

A shadow organization embedded inside governments worldwide. Their mission: keep the hidden world hidden at any cost. They erase evidence, control media, eliminate threats — and recruit investigators like you.

🐾
Restorer Veiled
Supremacists · Ancient Order

Veiled extremists who want to restore the ancient era when their kind openly hunted humans and wielded magic freely. A direct threat to the fragile peace the Council has maintained for centuries.

🧿
The Attuned
Human · Veil-Sensitive · Dangerous

Rare humans who learned to connect with the Veil. They can see semi-revealed Veiled and sense residual energy from rituals. Most bear visible marks from the dangerous attuning process. The strongest can open microfissures.

🗡️
Grimm Royals
Hybrid · Elite · Unstoppable

Created through generations of strategic marriages. They are the strongest beings in the hidden world — and unlike Royal Veiled, their power does not tear the Veil. They are its antibody.

// 02 — About the Game

You See What
Others Cannot.

Beyond the Veil is an early-stage open-world ARPG prototype. You play as an investigator navigating the collision of two worlds — the mundane city above, and the supernatural underworld hidden beneath perception.

The core loop blends investigation gameplay — gathering evidence, tracking suspects, building cases — with real-time ARPG combat against Veiled creatures. Every case is a puzzle. Every confrontation is a risk.

This repository is the very beginning of that vision: city systems, player movement, and the architectural foundation for everything to come.

// 03 — Systems

Core Features

🗺️
Open World City

District-based urban map with free exploration — streets, alleys, rooftops, and locations where the Veil bleeds through. Designed to expand district by district as development progresses.

🔍
Investigation System

Examine crime scenes, collect evidence, and identify faction involvement. Build connections between clues in a dynamic case journal — the city speaks to those who know how to listen.

⚔️
ARPG Combat

Real-time combat with Veiled enemies in their revealed forms. Weapons, armor, accessories — you may have to fight a Royal one day. The hidden world does not reward the unprepared.

📔
Case Journal

Dynamic objective tracking, suspect boards, and evidence logs. Every case connects to the larger hidden war — some leads point much further than expected.

🧠
NPC & Dialogue

Humans, Veiled, Attuned, and Council agents each hold pieces of the truth. Not every answer comes willingly — and some sources have their own agenda.

📈
Progression & Loot

Upgrades through solved cases and combat. Equipment with rarity tiers, including rare Veil-infused items. Build your investigator before the factions notice you exist.

// 04 — Story

Prologue:
"The Skalengek Case"

01
The Hunt

Night. A rain-soaked city. A Veiled investigator working for the Council of Silence tracks a Skalengek criminal — one killing in plain sight and using transformation to make witnesses look insane.

02
The Crime Scene

Claw marks. Distorted cameras. Terrified witnesses saying they saw a monster. She follows the pattern, predicts the next strike — and gets there before the Council finishes erasing the evidence.

03
The Chase

She intercepts the Skalengek. He runs, laughing. The pursuit tears through narrow streets, fire escapes, and abandoned lots — until it ends somewhere no witnesses can follow.

04
The Fight & The Fall

He fights dirty. Psychological pressure, traps, a cowardly blow. She falls. He approaches slowly — confident he has won. The camera holds on his slow footsteps.

05
Footsteps in the Silence

A sound. Distant. Growing closer. The Skalengek stops. The air changes. She cannot stand — but something is coming. Something that makes even a killer go still.

06–09
The Grimm — & What Comes Next

A man appears. Calm. Kanabō over one shoulder. He is the legend Veiled parents use to scare children. Now he is real — and she has seen him. That changes everything.

// Scene 06 — Fragment

A man appeared at the far end of the lot.

The first impression was simple: an ordinary human. Calm. Unhurried. One hand in his pocket — the other carrying a kanabō over his shoulder like a baseball bat he forgot to put down.

He walked like someone with nowhere to be.

The Skalengek snarled. Then transformed — ready to terrify, ready to end this.

And then he saw.

Eyes completely black. Dark lines tracing the edges. And in that darkness — a reflection. Not of the man standing there. Of himself. Every predatory impulse. Every worst thing he was.

Fear arrived before thought did.

She forced her own transformation through the pain — just to understand what was happening. And she saw what she had been told did not exist.

A Grimm.

// 05 — Gameplay Loop

How a Case Unfolds

01
Receive Report

A call, a Council assignment, or something you stumble across. Something happened that humans cannot explain.

02
Investigate

Reach the scene. Collect evidence. See what the Veil left behind that others miss.

03
Follow Leads

Track suspects across the city. Talk. Observe. Identify the faction behind the case.

04
Confront

When words fail, the fight begins. Some things reveal themselves — and some don't stop.

05
Collect & Prepare

Rewards, loot, reputation. The city grows more dangerous the more you learn about it.
